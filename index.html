What we do!

We build Portable security cabins, Toilets, Site Office, Conference hall, Kitchen, Large site Offices, G+ 1 Resorts, Farm Houses, E-House, Bunk House, Tool Booths, KISOKS, portable ATM's, Site labs, Security Chowkies in Defense areas, Ticketing counters, Portable Temple & Portable Schools.

Our portable units are spacious and uniquely designed with aesthetic interiors to make one fill comfortable.

All internal accessories are of standard make & ISI mark.

We use branded materials to manufacture our portable cabins.

We provide customization to end user for faster occupancy, so you can get back to business.

The main construction of our portable cabins consist of specially formed GI(Galvanized Iron) Channels, Side Post, Stiffeners & Corrugated Sheets.

Well insulated cabins with rock wool Slabs which resists the heat.

Internal walls, decorative paneling of MDF & E-Board with False Celling.
